Hangry Dog Game

The Hangry Dog Game is designed for players aged 6 and up.

In this game, the player controls a dog character who must collect food items while avoiding or destroying bad items (such as Dog poo, though currently only evil easter eggs are available). The first level of the game lasts for 1 minute, during which time the player must eat as much food as possible and avoid/destroy as many bad items as possible. Once the first level is completed, additional levels may be developed.
