Hangry Dog Game

The Hangry Dog Game is designed for players aged 6 and up.

In this game, the player controls a dog character who must destroy bad items (such as Dog poo, though currently only evil easter eggs are available), different kinds of enemies will be presented. The first level of the game lasts for 1 minute, during which time the player must destroy as many bad items as possible. Once the first level is completed, additional levels may be developed with increased enemies speed.
